Patient privacy

✓

Everything runs on your computer

Patient data stays on your machine. Nothing is sent to external servers. The interfaces are fully functional and ready for real clinical data.

⚠

Don't paste patient data into the chat

The conversation with Claude is processed by AI servers. Enter patient data directly into the interfaces Claude builds for you — that stays on your computer.

🛡

Claude watches out for you

If anything you ask could create a privacy risk — like connecting to an external service or exposing data — Claude will warn you before proceeding and explain the risk in plain language.

  • No external database connections by default
  • No data uploaded to any cloud service
  • To deploy on a network or connect to a database, consult your compliance team first

What you don't need to worry about

  • ✓ You will never be asked about technical details — the system decides those for you
  • ✓ You won't see code, error messages, or terminal output unless you ask
  • ✓ If something isn't right, just describe what you'd like changed
  • ✓ The system uses established clinical standards automatically
