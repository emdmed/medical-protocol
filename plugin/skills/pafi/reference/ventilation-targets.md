# Ventilation Targets for ARDS

## Lung-Protective Ventilation (ARDSNet Protocol)

| Parameter | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Tidal volume | 6 mL/kg predicted body weight (PBW) |
| Plateau pressure | ≤ 30 cmH₂O |
| Respiratory rate | 6–35 breaths/min (adjust to pH) |
| SpO₂ target | 88–95% |
| pH target | 7.30–7.45 |

## PEEP/FiO₂ Combinations (ARDSNet Low PEEP Table)

| FiO₂ | 0.3 | 0.4 | 0.5 | 0.6 | 0.7 | 0.8 | 0.9 | 1.0 |
|-------|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| PEEP | 5 | 5–8 | 8–10 | 10 | 10–14 | 14 | 14–18 | 18–24 |

## Predicted Body Weight (PBW)

- **Male:** 50 + 0.91 × (height in cm − 152.4) kg
- **Female:** 45.5 + 0.91 × (height in cm − 152.4) kg

## Oxygenation Goals by Severity

| Severity | PaO₂ Target | SpO₂ Target | Strategy |
|----------|------------|------------|----------|
| Mild | > 80 mmHg | > 95% | Low PEEP, minimize FiO₂ |
| Moderate | 60–80 mmHg | 88–95% | Moderate PEEP, prone positioning consider |
| Severe | 55–80 mmHg | 88–95% | High PEEP, prone positioning, consider ECMO |

## Rescue Therapies

- **Prone positioning** — for PaO₂/FiO₂ < 150 mmHg despite optimal ventilation (≥ 16 hours/day)
- **Neuromuscular blockade** — consider in first 48 hours for severe ARDS
- **ECMO** — consider when PaO₂/FiO₂ < 80 despite optimized ventilation
